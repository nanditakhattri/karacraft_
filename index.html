Our Story

Kara Craft was born in the heart of Kathmandu, where ancient craftsmanship meets contemporary elegance. Each piece is lovingly handcrafted by skilled artisans who transform precious metals and gemstones into wearable art. We believe in slow fashion - creating jewelry that tells stories, celebrates traditions, and becomes part of your journey. From our hands to yours, every piece carries the warmth of handmade perfection and the rich heritage of Nepali craftsmanship.
